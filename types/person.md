# Person

A Person is a human participant who owns, stewards, uses, or shapes part of the environment.

## When to use it

Use this type only when a human role matters to understanding responsibility or stewardship.

## Examples

- [[Kevin]]

## Not to be confused with

Do not confuse a Person with an AI system, assistant, or interface.
