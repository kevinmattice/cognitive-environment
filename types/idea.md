# Idea

An Idea is a possible concept, interpretation, or direction that is not yet accepted architecture.

## When to use it

Use this type for exploratory concepts, hypotheses, and open possibilities.

## Examples

- A future modeling concept still under discussion

## Not to be confused with

Do not confuse an Idea with an adopted decision, an operational system, or a settled fact. If a note is uncertain, status `Proposal` may also be appropriate.
