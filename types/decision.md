# Decision

A Decision is a recorded choice that sets direction or closes an important question.

## When to use it

Use this type when it is important to preserve what was chosen and why.

## Examples

- A future decision about atlas scope or naming

## Not to be confused with

Do not confuse a Decision with an idea under discussion or with a task that carries the decision out.
